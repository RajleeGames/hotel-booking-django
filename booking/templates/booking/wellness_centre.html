OUR FACILITIES

FITNESS CENTRE

Both business and leisure travelers will find a welcoming sanctuary away from daily pressures at our Health and Fitness Club. The luxurious enclave offers harmonious journeys of purification and rejuvenation for both body and soul.

On the second floor, Kibo’s Health and Fitness Club is in an environment that inspires our guests and members to perform, featuring top-of-the-range amenities that keep them energized under the supervision of our professional instructors.

MASSAGE CENTRE

Take indulgence to a new level by treating your body with our massage treatments designed for rest and rejuvenation. Counter the negative effects of travel-induced stress with a stimulating massage to lull you deeper into relaxation—guaranteed to instill a sense of peace and wellbeing that is simply impossible to resist.

SAUNA OR STEAM ROOM

Your visit is not complete without a session—or several—in our sauna or steam room. Detox, relax, and loosen your muscles using the ancient method of cleansing and healing the body, mind, and spirit.

SWIMMING POOL

With access to the Tropical Bar, our pool—one of the best in Arusha—offers healthy juices, cocktails, and light revitalizing snacks, providing a welcome relief from the city’s buzz. As the night sets in, the pool morphs into an ambiance of chic relaxation. The pool is open to the general public, offering a decadent lounging experience.
